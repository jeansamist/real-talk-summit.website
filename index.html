Meet Lisa Walker —
The Heart Behind
Real Talk Summit

Lisa Walker didn't start the Real Talk Summit because everything in her life was perfect. She started it because she knew too many people were quietly suffering — in their marriages, in their singlehood, in their grief — while wearing a Sunday smile.

Driven by a deep conviction that the church should be a place of honest, healing conversation, Lisa created a space where real families could have real talks, grounded in biblical truth and practical wisdom.

Her message is simple and unwavering: restoration is possible. Whether you've walked through divorce, loss, betrayal, or simply the slow wear of life — there is a path forward, and you don't have to walk it alone.

— Lisa Walker
