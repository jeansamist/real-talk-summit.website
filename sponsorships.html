Where Your Money Goes

What Your Dollars Actually Do

Transparency is a value, not a marketing line. Here's how every sponsorship dollar is deployed to build the summit experience.

35%

Speaker & Program

Covers honorariums, travel, and materials for our anointed speaker lineup — making world-class voices accessible to every attendee.

25%

Venue & Operations

Ensures Faith Temple Church of God is set up perfectly — audio, lighting, seating, signage, and the team that makes it flow.

20%

Attendee Experience

Goodie bags, printed materials, registration, childcare coordination, and the hospitality that makes attendees feel truly welcomed.

20%

Marketing & Outreach

Digital advertising, social campaigns, and community outreach to ensure the right people — those who need it most — hear about this event.
